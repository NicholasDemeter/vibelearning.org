The Vibe Learning Framework

1. SENSE

Use AI to read participant energy, engagement levels, and emerging themes. Analyze group dynamics in real-time to inform adaptive strategies.

2. ADAPT

Modify content, activities, and pacing based on observed needs. Leverage AI to generate relevant materials and exercises on demand.

3. CO-CREATE

Involve participants in building solutions together. Use AI to structure real challenges into workable frameworks and action plans.

4. CAPTURE

Document outputs in immediately usable formats. Transform workshop activities into tangible resources that serve partner interests.
